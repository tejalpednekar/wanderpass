About


WanderPASS was created with one mission in mind: to make international travel simpler, clearer, and more accessible for every U.S. citizen. We know that planning a trip can be overwhelming—especially when it comes to understanding visa requirements. Different rules, changing regulations, and scattered information often turn excitement into stress. WanderPASS exists to change that.

Our platform brings visa information for destinations around the world into one easy-to-use space. Instead of searching through multiple websites or government portals, travelers can quickly check whether they need a visa in just a few seconds.

WanderPASS is built for curious explorers, first-time travelers, and seasoned adventurers alike. Whether you're planning a solo journey, a family vacation, or a multi-country itinerary, we’re here to help you move confidently and without confusion.

We believe travel should inspire, not intimidate. With accurate information and a seamless experience, WanderPASS empowers you to discover the world with clarity and ease.

Your next adventure starts here — and we’re with you every step of the way.
